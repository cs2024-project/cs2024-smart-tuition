TEACHERS
Ms Clare Tan

A former teacher from various top institutions in Singapore, Clare believes that there can always be more to be done to enrich students' experience in embracing the English language in their everyday lives. Clare believes that English should be taught as a life skill, not as a examinable subject. With most students improving by 3 grades minimally on average, Clare believes that every student is worth the lesson.

Mr Kim Seng

Combining his passion of teaching and Mathematics, Kim Seng aspires to guide students through the syllabus, while pushing them further and beyond where they stand. Kim Seng believes that Mathematics isn't as dry as what many may believe and every kid will find their way to properly understanding the subject. Proudly owning a 100% PSLE A rating, Kim Seng promises to make Mathematics interesting and understood by your kids.

Mr Tom Lim

A former Physics Olympiad trainer, Tom aspires to push every individual student to their limits while nurturing their love for Science. With his experience with students who had struggled with the understanding of complex scientific theories, Tom always tries his best to make them bite-sized for your kids' understanding.

Ms Jenny Kim

An Information Engineering and Media graduate, Jenny aspires to bring kids to the magical world of coding to kickstart them into the technological world of the 21st century. With her area of focus being object oriented programming, she aims to bring students through the ins and outs of languages including C and Java.

Ms Judy Carter

With dreams to bring out everyone's best and most creative talents, Judy aims to utilise her 20 years of artistic experiences into helping students find a new form of expression. With former students winning HCBS banks' annual art contest at the young age of 10, she believes that there is no boundary to creativity and she believes that creative minds should be nurtured to its full potential.

Ms Margaret Nicholls

Having worked for big name musicals including Hamilton, Hairspray and Les Miserables, Margaret works on having your child learn how to express, how to enact and how to empathise. Through her lessons, students will learn how to be both a cast member of an act while also function as part of a crew. Many parents have testified that their child has become much more outgoing with Margaret around.
