ABOUT US
Mr Joseph Lam
FOUNDER

Joseph believes that education has a simple and sole goal - to teach and for learning. He believes that everything can be made interesting to the young developing minds of kids and he believes that learning materials is everywhere. He then launched SMART with one single goal in mind, to provide a safe and conducive environment for his students.

Ms Lorna Morello
CO-FOUNDER, OPERATIONS MANAGER

Growing up in a family of educators, Lorna believes that there can always be more given to every individual in their early stage of learning. She believes in the power of empathetic teaching and she believes that each child should be taught in unique manners, for the simple reason that each individual kid is unique.

Mr Arjun Sharma
CEO

On a teaching journey in a volun-tourism trip to Nepal, Arjun saw genuine shine in every kids' eyes as he taught them pythagoras theorem, what would otherwise be perceived as boring by most. Through this, he realised that the process of learning should be imparted as what it is, and not a chore. Hence, he joined hands with Joseph on the journey of Smart to make learning the best childhood memory of many children out there.

Ms Melinda Alexis Smith-Kim
HEAD OF TUTOR TRAINING

When it comes to producing results, Melinda is the person who assures it in the day to day operation of SMART, affectionately called the "principal" of the center, she ensures that every student has a wonderful experience learning while also keeping every parent updated and assured that their child is doing well. Mathilda ensures every teacher and student alike are pushed to their limits and beyond.
