Our center is MOE approved and all our teachers were head-hunted from prestigious MOE schools, or their respective fields. Smart is also awarded the Parents' Choice Best Center for 3 consecutive years and Students' Choice Best Center for 2 consecutive years, amongst our recorded number of 100 awards. Students have testified to have enjoyed the process of learning under Smart, while also improved tremendously in enthusiasm and results alike. With center exclusive worksheets, notes and tests, be assured that your students are in good hands.
