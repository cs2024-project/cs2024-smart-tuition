OUR CURRICULUM (Enrichment)

CODING

To prepare your child for the technology age in 21st century, we provide coding classes that offers to teach your child industrial standard languages, design based languages and coding logics.


Level 1 & 2

As an introduction, students will first learn HTML and CSS as a way to build their own website and write blogspots. At this level, students will:

  • Learn HTML (level 1) and CSS (level 2)
  • Build engaging websites to record their personal life
  • Get their designs selected to be the web design for our English Essay blog!

Level 3 & 4

At this level, students can begin to upgrade their website by learning the ropes to building a back-end with forms and filters.

  • Learn PHP (level 3) and Javascript (level 4)
  • Have experts visits to talk about building a solid website
  • Case studies with different industries and their need monthly

Level 5 & 6

At level 5 and 6, students will be able to move on from web design to learn object oriented languages. By the end of level 6, students will be able to walk out with their own premade games and applications.

  • Learn C & Java (level 5) and Android Studio (level 6)
  • Build their own versions of classical games (Sudoku and Minesweeper)
  • Learn how to write pseudocodes and the logic behind object oriented programming
  • Build their own simple game with logics in Scratch
  • Build a functional Android App by the end of level 6

SPEECH AND DRAMA

As a way to bring up your child's literacy skills, we offer Speech and Drama classes to encourage your child into a new form of self-expression. Students will be taught to improve on articulation, clarity and be exposed to various forms of theaters through the level. Students will also put together a play at the end of the year.


Level 1 & 2

In the beginning level, students will be encouraged to express themselves clearly at a comfortable level while focusing on eununciation and clarity of speech. Students will also be guided through word choices and expressions to convey a certain message.

  • Basic phonics and pronunciation
  • Sciript reading (basic level)
  • Improv and pretend play
  • Year-end play as a class

Level 3 & 4

At this level, students will begin to experience other forms of Theater and also other similar mediums such as Stand-up Comedy. Students will also get basic exposure to song and dance as part of a short musical curriculum.

  • Script reading and improv workshops
  • Class musical viewing by Pangdemonium! with all expenses paid.
  • Prop making and literary significance teaching
  • Year-end musical as a class

Level 5 & 6

By the end of their Speech and Drama training, students will be able to experience more abstract forms of theater such as Epic Theater and Forum Theater, students will also be participating in a center-wide speech contest with a common topic.

  • Script reading and improv workshops
  • Blocking, lighting and sound workshop
  • Prop making and literary significancec teaching
  • Speech contest
  • Year-end performance as a class

ART
