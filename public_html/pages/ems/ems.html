OUR CURRICULUM

ENGLISH

Our English curriculum offers a beyond-the-classroom promise whereby students will not only fare and score after attending our classes, but also be extremely fluent beyond the books. Students will be able to learn standard English as per MOE standards, with fully trained teachers from NIE.


P1 & 2

At a foundational level, students will be exposed to the Primary English syllabus through play and engaging worksheets. Through the use of art, vivid colours, we aim to ease students, English and Mother-tongue speaking, into the wonderful world of English. You can expect:

  • Worksheets relating to daily life and general knowledge, two birds, one stone!
  • Grammar drills and center-exclusive spelling lists.
  • Engaging plays (i.e crosswords and wordsearches) to pique students' interest in the subject.
  • Creative writing practices through story-telling, story-writing and monthly show-and-tells.

P3 & 4

In these years, students will be further honed on their Grammatical skills while continually expanding their Vocabulary exposure. Students will begin preparation for upper primary through drills. You can expect:

  • Monthly Tests.
  • Center-exclusive spelling & dictation lists and weekly grammar checks.
  • Alternating weeks of Paper 1 & 2 drills.
  • Literature exposure through books: Just Grace; Still Just Grace and Just Grace Walks the Dog by Charise Mericle Harper (P3) & Charlotte's Web by E.B. White (P4)

P5 & 6

In their final years of primary school, we will focus on getting students ready for the PSLE examinations, while continuing nurturing their love and passion for the language. As part of the English preparatory programme, we will:

  • Conduct monthly tests.
  • Offer complimentary extra classes tackling specific sections. (limited slots!)
  • Literature exposure through books: Sing to the Dawn by Minfong Ho (P5 - with free movie screening) & Chinese Cinderella by Adeline Yen Mah
  • Newspaper reading practices with exam-format practice exposures (i.e comprehension cloze, reading comprehension)
  • Alternating weeks of Paper 1 & 2 drills.
  • Analysis on areas of weakness.

Pssst... Our English class is proven to have increased effectiveness in both students' grades and interest when signed up with our Speech and Drama courses.

MATHEMATICS

Our Mathematics curriculum offers a less intimidating approach to Mathematics for weaker students by showcasing students the magic of numbers. Students will be trained to adapt to problem sums with enough practice.


P1 & 2

To invite kids into the subject, lower primary Mathematics will be guided through play, students will be drilled while being taught to enjoy Mathematics in the process. You can expect:

  • Drills on basic arithmetic skills (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division)
  • Monthly H.O.T questions with rewards
  • Exposure to abacus and mental calculation techniques.
  • Weekly timed revision at the end of the class
  • Topic coverage:
    • Whole Numbers
    • Arithmetics
    • Patterns
    • Fractions
    • Money
    • Length, Mass and Volume
    • Time
    • Shapes
    • Graphs

P3 & 4

Mathematics can begin to appear intimidating here, hence, Smart will ensure that students are comfortable with numbers at this stage, with appropriate practices and confident-boosters. Students will ace Mathematics in no time. Our curriuclum includes:

  • Weekly Quizzes on previous week topics
  • Monthly H.O.T questions with rewards.
  • Precision practice.
  • Leveled problem sum questions
  • Topic coverage:
    • Whole Numbers
    • Arithmetics
    • Patterns
    • Fractions & Decimals
    • Money
    • Length, Mass & Volume
    • Time
    • Shapes, Perimeter and Area
    • Lines & Angles
    • Graphs

P5 & 6

In their final years of primary school, we will focus on getting the students to more critically think, while also ensuring basic skills and accuracy is honed. As part of the Mathematics preparatory programme, we will:

  • Conduct monthly tests.
  • Offer complimentary extra classes tackling specific topics. (limited slots!)
  • Offer center-exclusive tough problem sum questions as part of the training
  • Alternating weeks of Paper 1 & 2 drills.
  • Analysis on areas of weakness.
  • Topic coverage:
    • Whole Numbers
    • Arithmetics
    • Patterns
    • Fractions, Ratios, Percentage & Decimals
    • Rate
    • Money
    • Length, Mass & Volume
    • Time
    • Shapes, Perimeter and Area
    • Lines & Angles
    • Graphs
    • Average

Pssst... Our Mathematics class works best hand-in-hand on logic based techniques with our Coding courses.

SCIENCE

As a new subject to primary 3 students, we aim to have students learn critical scientific techniques while also nurture their creativity to the world around us.


P3 & 4 (Lower block)

As a new subject, we aim to, at this stage, ease students into the wonders of science and hone answering techniques at an early stage.

  • Monthly experiment demonstrations.
  • Monthly H.O.T questions with rewards.
  • Center exclusive science spellings.
  • Answering technique guidelines.
  • Topic coverage:
    • Diversity
    • Systems
    • Cycles
    • Energy
    • Interactions of Forces (Magnets)

P5 & 6

Where most students fail to grasp proper answering techniques, Smart aims to assist students in forming coherent scientific answers, while revisiting previous topics and breaking concepts into bite-sized pieces.

  • Conduct monthly tests.
  • Offer complimentary extra classes tackling specific topics. (limited slots!)
  • Offer center-exclusive commonly tested conceptual questions.
  • Analysis on areas of weakness.
  • Q&A hotline.
  • Topic coverage:
    • Cycles (Reproduction & Water Cycles)
    • Systems
    • Interactions of Forces
    • Energy
    • Interactions with the Environment

Pssst... Our Art and Craft classes offer model building with scientific explanation once a month to assist kids in better visualising scientific concepts.
