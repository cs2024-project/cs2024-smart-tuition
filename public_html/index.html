PSLE Foundational Subjects

Our English PSLE curriculum is a proven successful model of teaching through years of experimentation and refinement. Every linguistic technique taught is tailored with time and moving with it.

Under our English curriculum, your student will see significant improvement in their reading, writing and Paper 2 techniques.
Our Mathematics curriculum has been one that is proven to help formerly weaker students understand the complicated process of question solving, and to begin visualising models. Currently strong students are given the extra push they need for the examination, or even, a step further into secondary school.

We focus on conceptual understanding, breaking thought processes into digestible pieces and in accuracy.
Our Science syllabus keeps students constantly on their toes on scientific concepts learnt throughout the year, by relating the concepts to real life. This prompts students to always ask questions to better understand.

We also have emphasis in answering techniques, to help students gain the points usually lost in Booklet B. Through our science lessons, students will be able to understand concepts, answering techniques while walking out with observational skills that will benefit them for life.
